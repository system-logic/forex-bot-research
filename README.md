# Forex Bot Research

Research prototype for an adaptive mid-term decision system in a non-stationary Forex environment.

## Focus

- Regime detection & environmental awareness  
- Risk & constraint-aware filters  
- Edge hypotheses in real market conditions  
- Monitoring & handling of model degradation
